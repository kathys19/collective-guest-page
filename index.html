Your Questions, Answered

Is it free to attend as a guest? +
Yes! Your first visit as a guest is completely free. We would love to welcome you and give you a genuine taste of the Sisters of the Heart Collective community — no pressure, no commitment, just an open invitation.
What is the Sisters of the Heart Collective? +
The Sisters of the Heart Collective is a warm, grace-filled community of women gathering monthly on Zoom, led by Co-Directors Janie Seltzer and Kathy A. Souza of the Women’s Center for SoulCARE. It is a place for personal & spiritual growth, encouragement & support, grace-filled relationships, and teaching from Janie’s signature framework. Simply put — You Belong Here!
What happens during the gathering? +
Each gathering includes teaching from Janie’s signature framework, time for community connection, and encouragement and support. It is a simple, grounding 75-minute experience on Zoom — designed to nourish your soul and connect you with kindred women walking a similar path.
Do I need to be a member to attend? +
No! Your first gathering is completely free as our guest. After that, membership opens the door to ongoing community, connection, and growth with the Sisters of the Heart Collective.
How long is the meeting? +
Each gathering is 75 minutes — enough time to go deep, connect meaningfully, and leave feeling nourished without taking over your whole day.
